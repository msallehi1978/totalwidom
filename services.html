Industrial Network Security

Securing the Backbone of Critical Infrastructure

Industrial networks — OT/ICS/SCADA/DCS — form the backbone of critical infrastructure in oil & gas, petrochemicals, power generation, water utilities, transportation, and manufacturing. Any cyber-attack or operational disruption may lead to production downtime, financial loss, and safety hazards.

Aligned with IEC 62443 and NIST SP 800-82, our services provide a comprehensive framework for securing industrial networks and ensuring resilient operations.

Methodology

Standards-Based Framework

Our methodology follows global best practices and sector-specific standards to ensure full compliance and maximum security posture.

IEC 62443

Security requirements for Industrial Automation and Control Systems (IACS), zone/conduit architecture, Security Levels (SL0–SL4), and Foundational Requirements (FR1–FR7).

NIST SP 800-82

Practical guidance for OT/ICS/SCADA cybersecurity risk management and security control implementation.

NIST CSF 2.0

Cybersecurity Framework covering Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover functions across the organization.

IEC 62351

Security for communication in power systems including IEC 61850 protocols and substation automation security.

ISO/IEC 27019

Information security controls specifically tailored for the energy sector and utility organizations (2024 edition).

NERC-CIP

Mandatory standards for critical infrastructure protection in energy — covering physical and cyber security requirements.

API 1164

Cybersecurity framework for pipeline SCADA systems, addressing risk management and security architecture.

NIS2 (EU)

Governance and reporting requirements for critical industries operating in or connected to European markets.

Core Services

What We Deliver

Six comprehensive service domains designed to address every dimension of industrial cybersecurity.

Assessment & Risk Analysis

  • Asset discovery and inventory of OT/ICS devices and communication flows
  • Network zoning and conduit definition
  • Cyber risk assessment and Target Security Level (SL-T) setting
  • GAP Analysis based on IEC 62443 maturity model

Security Architecture & Design

  • Layered architecture based on the Purdue Enterprise Reference Model
  • Segmentation through zones and conduits with defined trust boundaries
  • Industrial DMZ design and IT/OT traffic segregation
  • Selection and design of industrial-grade security controls (firewalls, IDS/IPS, data diodes)

Implementation & Hardening

  • Deployment and configuration of industrial firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Hardening of PLCs, HMIs, RTUs, and engineering workstations
  • Identity and access management (IAM, MFA, role-based access)
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest, plus secure key management

Monitoring & Incident Response

  • Deployment of industrial SIEM and SOC for OT environments
  • Continuous log collection, anomaly detection, and threat intelligence integration
  • Development of Incident Response Playbooks tailored for OT/ICS
  • Red Team/Blue Team and Table-top exercises for resilience testing

Supply Chain Security

  • Supplier security assessment and compliance (IEC 62443-4-1, NERC-CIP-013)
  • Secure Development Lifecycle (SDL) and patch management processes

Policy, Training & Audit

  • Development of security policies and OT-specific procedures
  • Workforce training and awareness programs for operators and engineers
  • Periodic audits and continuous improvement reviews
